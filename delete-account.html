AutoMarket Pro – Account & Data Deletion

Last Updated: March 2026

At AutoMarket Pro, we respect your privacy and provide users with the ability to delete their account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1 – From Inside the App:

  1. Log in to your AutoMarket Pro account.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.

Option 2 – By Email:

If you cannot access your account, you may request deletion by contacting:
Email: automarketpro@gmail.com

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profile information (name, email, avatar)
  • Car listings you created
  • Favorites
  • In-app messages and conversations
  • Location data associated with your account

Data Retention

Some limited information may be retained for up to 30 days for:

  • Fraud prevention
  • Security investigations
  • Legal compliance requirements

After the retention period, all remaining personal data is permanently deleted from our systems.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • You will lose access to all listings, messages, and saved data.
  • If you signed in using Google, your Google account itself is not deleted.
