Privacy Policy for AutoMarket Pro

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

AutoMarket Pro operates primarily in Algeria and facilitates international vehicle sourcing and import transactions, including vehicles sourced from China and other jurisdictions.

This Privacy Policy explains how AutoMarket Pro collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data of users in Algeria and internationally.

By using the Platform, you consent to data processing as described herein.

2. Data We Collect

Account Information (Supabase Authentication)

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • National ID (if required for transactions)

Account authentication and secure login are managed through Supabase authentication services.

Transaction Data

  • Vehicle purchase inquiries
  • Import documentation
  • Communication records
  • Uploaded vehicle images and related documents

Images & Camera Access

We may access the device camera and photo library to allow users to upload vehicle photos, documentation, or profile images. Images are stored securely and used solely for transaction and platform functionality purposes. Images are not shared for advertising purposes.

Location Data

We may collect approximate location data only when users actively use specific location-based features within the app. Location access is optional and requires user permission. We do not continuously track precise location in the background, and location data is not shared with third parties for advertising.

Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • App usage analytics
  • Log files

Analytics data does not include personal identifiers and is used solely to improve performance and security.

3. How We Use Data

We process data to:

  • Provide Platform services
  • Facilitate vehicle sourcing and international import transactions
  • Communicate with users
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
  • Comply with Algerian and international legal obligations
  • Improve services and user experience

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contractual necessity
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interest
  • User consent

5. Data Sharing

We may share data only where necessary with:

  • Logistics providers
  • Customs brokers
  • Government authorities
  • Legal advisors
  • Technology service providers (including Supabase infrastructure)
  • Future payment service providers (when integrated)

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data for advertising purposes.

6. International Transfers

Because vehicles may be sourced internationally, personal data may be transferred outside Algeria where necessary to complete transactions. Appropriate safeguards are implemented to protect such transfers.

7. Data Retention

  • As long as required for service provision
  • As required by Algerian law
  • As necessary for dispute resolution

8. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard encryption protocols. We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data.

However, no system guarantees absolute security.
